Our Dental Treatments

Comprehensive solutions for all your dental needs.

Teeth Cleaning & Scaling

Professional removal of plaque and tartar to prevent gum disease and maintain oral hygiene.

Tooth Filling

Restoring the function and structure of decayed or damaged teeth using aesthetic filling materials.

Tooth Extraction

Safe removal of non-restorable teeth with minimal discomfort using advanced techniques.

Artificial Complete Denture

Full set of removable teeth for patients who have lost all their natural teeth.

Dental Implants

Permanent solution for missing teeth that look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive procedures for gum treatments and surgeries using advanced laser technology.

Root Canal Therapy

Saving a severely decayed or infected tooth by removing the damaged pulp and sealing it.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Surgical removal of impacted or problematic wisdom teeth to prevent pain and crowding.

Fixed Partial Denture

Bridges used to replace one or more missing teeth, permanently attached to adjacent teeth.

Teeth Whitening (Bleaching)

Professional whitening treatments to brighten your smile and remove stubborn stains.

Veneers

Thin shells of porcelain or composite to improve the appearance of stained or chipped teeth.

Pediatric Dentistry

Specialized dental care for children, ensuring a positive and friendly experience.

Flap Surgery

Advanced gum treatment to clean deep pockets and treat periodontal disease.

Orthodontic Braces

Traditional metal or ceramic braces to correct misaligned teeth and bite issues.

Clear Aligners

Invisible, removable aligners for a discreet way to straighten your teeth.
