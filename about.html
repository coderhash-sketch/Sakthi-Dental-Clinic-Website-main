About Sakthi Dental Clinic

Two decades of excellence in dental care.

Get to Know Dr. Anupriya

Founder & Senior Dental Surgeon

Dr. Anupriya is a highly experienced dental professional with over 20 years of dedicated practice in the field of dentistry. She founded Sakthi Dental Clinic in 2004 with a vision to provide high-quality, compassionate dental care to the community of Hosur.

Throughout her career, she has been deeply involved in community service, having served at the Primary Health Center, where she provided essential dental services to underserved populations. Her patient-centric approach and commitment to excellence have made Sakthi Dental Clinic a trusted name for families in the region.

She specializes in comprehensive dental care for women and children, ensuring a comfortable and stress-free experience for even the most anxious patients.

Our Vision

To be the leading dental care provider in Hosur, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative technology, and compassionate patient care, creating healthy smiles for generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide personalized, high-quality dental care in a welcoming environment. We strive to educate our patients about oral health and utilize the latest advancements in dentistry to deliver superior results.
